### Covered

- 903,000+ 7(a) small-business loans
- Aggregates by lender, county, state, and NAICS industry
- Charge-off and default outcomes

### Known limitation — Connecticut FIPS schema change

A 2022 FIPS schema change for Connecticut counties left approximately **6,408 loans** unresolved at the county level in the current ingest. These loans are visible at the state level but do not roll up into county-level aggregates. Fix targeted for the next data refresh.
