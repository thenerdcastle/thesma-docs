### Covered

- **CES** — industry employment (monthly, seasonally adjusted)
- **QCEW** — county wages and employment by industry
- **OEWS** — occupation-level wages and employment
- **JOLTS** — job openings and labor turnover
- **LAUS** — state-level unemployment rates

### Not yet covered (post-1.0.0 roadmap)

CPI, PPI, ECI, productivity statistics, and NCS are **not** in the 1.0.0 release. If any of these are load-bearing for your use case, let us know at [hello@thesma.dev](mailto:hello@thesma.dev) — it informs prioritisation.
