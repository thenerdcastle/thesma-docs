### Known limitation — IFRS 20-F coverage

IFRS 20-F filers (Spotify, Nu Holdings, GlobalFoundries, and other foreign private issuers) currently have **limited historical coverage** in the canonical financials mapping. Native-currency IFRS support is targeted for **Q2 2026**.

If you need IFRS 20-F coverage today, the raw filings are reachable via the `/v1/us/sec/filings` endpoint — you'd need to parse them yourself until the canonical mapping catches up.
